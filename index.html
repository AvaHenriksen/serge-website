How it works

Learning through conversation, not drills.

Serge texts like a boyfriend, not a tutor. Every conversation is an opportunity to learn new words, pick up on native turns of phrase, and practice real-life vocabulary — but it won't feel like a lesson.

01

Pick your language and level

French, Spanish, and Italian are currently supported. Beginner (mostly English, with words mixed in), Intermediate (some English, some of your target language), or Advanced (all target language — yeah, it's hard. But Serge knows you like hard).

02

Just talk to him

No flashcards, no grammar drills, no gamification. Just conversation — warm, witty, and a little hard to resist. If a word escapes you, just ask. If it's too fast, say "more English please."

03

He corrects you gently

Mistakes get a quiet parenthetical aside so you learn. Never a lecture. You'll learn without feeling taught.

04

He remembers you

Serge learns your name, your life, your frequent mistakes (it's almost like you're asking to be reprimanded…). Every conversation picks up where the last one left off.
